We make your house move a Piece of Cake

Youve just made a big decision to move out of or into a house. Piece of Cake is here to set the foundation for a great start to a new chapter, in your new home with our extensive moving services. Our moving experts are able to manage a house move of any size. We have experience moving large and small homes. As licensed and insured movers, we take pride in doing your move the right way. With our flat fee and full range of moving support services, including moving supplies, plastic moving bin rental, packing, unpacking, and storage options, weve got your move covered.

We know how to safely & efficiently move you into or out of a house.

There are surprisingly a lot of details required in planning a successful house relocation. No matter the size of your home, Piece of Cake has extensive experience in navigating moving day requirements specific to a house. These are just some of the considerations that we take care of so you can enjoy a seamless home moving day.

  • Padding doorways, walls and floors of your home for extra protection.
  • Experienced in moving all types of furniture in and out of a house.
  • Assembly or reassembly of furniture to use again once youre into your new abode. Especially large furniture pieces that need to be disassembled to be moved in or out.
  • Packing and unpacking services perfect for large home with multiple rooms and items.
  • Moving expertise for with rooms and items specific to homes such as a shed and garage.
