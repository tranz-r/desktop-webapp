Frequently Asked Los Angeles Storage Questions

What is pick up & drop off On Demand Storage?

Pick up & drop off On-Demand Storage is when Piece of Cake Moving & Storage comes to your place, picks up your items for storage, and stores it safely for you in our storage facility. When you need your items back, just give us a call, and we’ll drop them off at your doorstep. The super convenient and hassle-free storage solution.

Does Piece of Cake offer storage services in LA?

Absolutely! We offer local Manhattan storage services tailored to meet all of your storage needs.

Whether you’re caught in a bind and require Emergency Manhattan Storage for just a few days or up to two weeks, we’ve got your back. If you’re between places and need a short-term solution, our Manhattan Short-Term Storage service has you covered. Or our Long Term Manhattan Storage allows the flexibility to store your belongings for as long as you need – a few months or years. The choice is entirely yours!

Does Piece of Cake offer packing services for storage pick up in LA?

Definitely! We’ll handle the entire process for you, including packing your items for storage. Our team will professionally pack your items, and then move your possessions directly from your home to our storage facility. We also offer moving materials and boxes in our packing service.

How do I schedule a storage drop off when I need my items again?

Our skilled movers take care of the heavy lifting both during pick-up and when delivering your items back to you. Simply let us know when you’re ready, and we’ll redeliver your belongings. When you need your items back again, you can give us a call at (212) 651-7273 or fill out our storage drop off form. Our team will schedule your storage drop off at a date and time convenient for you.

How do I properly pack for storage?

When packing to have your items in storage you want to make sure you use the correct packing material and packing techniques to make sure that your items are safe and secure during their transit and time in storage. It’s crucial to start with quality packing materials. Check out our storage packing guide when packing your items for storage.

The benefits of using Piece of Cake On Demand storage service

We take your possessions straight to our storage facility. It means don’t have to worry about moving your items yourself to a storage facility, hiring a truck to move your items, finding a storage unit and traveling to a storage unit whenever you need your items back. We will pick up your items, pack them and move them and when you want them back just let us know for a drop off.

Here are some of the reasons to choose Piece of Cake’s full storage service

  • Affordable short and long term storage solutions based on the cubic foot of the items you are wanting to put into storage not an empty storage unit.

  • No lifting and hassle to move your items yourself. Our movers take care of that for you at pick up and delivery when you need your items back.

  • We will pick up, store and redeliver, with no need for you to coordinate the logistics.

  • Boxes available to purchase for pack your items for storage. We will drop these off 1 week before your storage pick up.

  • On demand drop off of your items – Just let us know when you need your belongings back and our movers will drop them off for you.

  • 24/7/36 security through personnel and constant video monitoring making sure your possessions are always accounted for

  • Clean storage facilities in the Tri State Area that are privately operated by Piece of Cake Moving & Storage for Piece of Cake customers only.

  • At no extra cost minimum DOT storage insurance on your items, with the option to purchase more insurance for your items while they are in storage.
